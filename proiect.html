Vivienne Westwood is a renowned British fashion brand founded by the iconic designer Vivienne Westwood in the 1970s. Known for its pioneering role in the punk fashion movement, the brand combines bold, rebellious aesthetics with a rich historical and cultural narrative.

Westwood’s designs often feature eclectic patterns, avant-garde silhouettes, and a distinct use of tartan, reflecting her deep connection to British heritage. The brand has continually pushed boundaries, merging high fashion with activism through its collections.

Heritage

The Yasmine Bag
The Yasmine Bag was originally named after the French designer Yasmine Eslami, who worked for the house during the late 80’s. The silhouette was inspired by the teardrop shape of a traditional knitting bag Yasmine would carry during this period, to hold her absolute essentials: cigarettes, a lighter and lipstick.
