Vivienne Westwood Spring 1998

The Spring 1998 Vivienne Westwood collection showcased a bold, eclectic mix of historical references and contemporary flair. The dresses were a striking blend of romanticism and rebellion, with corset-inspired structures, flowing skirts, and intricate draping.

The 2013 Vivienne Westwood collection featured corseted dresses with exaggerated bustiers, military-inspired jackets with wide shoulders, and asymmetric skirts in vibrant tartan and plaid. Oversized coats with uneven hems, tailored blazers, and deconstructed layers added a rebellious edge.
